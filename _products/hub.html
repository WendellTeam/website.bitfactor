Arctic HUB

Automate your cyber threat intelligence cycle

Watch video

Threat Intelligence Cycle

Arctic Hub enables you to automate your cyber threat intelligence cycle where raw threat data is turned into actionable threat intelligence. This ensures you get the threat information collected, harmonized and disseminated in a timely and effective manner. Read further to learn 7 steps for a modern cyber threat intelligence cycle and how Arctic Hub can help you implement them.

1

Collection

Cyber threat intelligence cycle starts with collecting threat data from a variety of threat indicator sources. In Arctic Hub you can configure all of these feeds and their properties.

2

Harmonization

Once you have collected the threat data you should harmonize the attributes into a unified language to create rules for the automation. When you call the same things by the same name and keep disparate things separate you turn the collected threat indicator data into threat information. Arctic Hub does all of this automatically for you.

3

Exploitation

Exploitation means a phase in the cyber threat intelligence cycle where network and geolocation related attributes as well as customer information are augmented to the original data set. Also, threat data is matched against customer configuration. To get Arctic Hub do all of this for you automatically you need to first configure your customers by defining their internet presence. This you can do easily in the tab called Customers in Arctic Hub.

4

Analysis

In the analysis phase threat information is matched against custom labeling rules. Moreover, custom tags are augmented into the original data set. When using Arctic Hub you can do all of this easily by using the custom labeling functionality.

5

Production

After analysis the threat intelligence packages are produced to be ready for delivery for the correct stakeholders. Arctic Hub can do all of this automatically for you once you have configured the sharing parameters in advance. You can select what kind of packages you want to create, which packages to share to which customers and whether to do the sharing via email reports or direct API access.

6

Dissemination

One of the most important phases in the threat intelligence cycle is to share the threat intelligence to the correct stakeholders. Once all the previous steps have been configured correctly in Arctic Hub no other actions are needed for the dissemination but this is automatically taken care of.

7

Feedback

Feedback helps you understand how the shared threat intelligence affects your stakeholders. With Arctic Hub you can see who of your customers access the information and when they are doing that.

Key Features

Automate notifications to your customers

Arctic Hub maps automatically the threat data to your customers based on their internet presence. You can also specify in a very detailed level on which kind of threat intelligence packages you want to send to each customer. Thus, your customers will get only the threat intelligence data that is valid for them. You can choose to send these via email reports or direct API access. This way your customers get the right information fast and can fix their issues quickly.

Understand your cyber resilience

Arctic Hub dashboard shows you real-time information on the threats your customers have faced. You can also see how the threat data has been divided by different type of customers or observation time. This gives you a good overall picture on the cyber resilience in your defense cell and you can follow up on the trends how your cyber resilience has improved over time.

Get feedback on how your customers use the data

Arctic Hub shows you who of your customers have accessed the threat intelligence packages you have sent to them and when they have done that. This helps you understand how effective your threat intelligence sharing is and if your customers find it useful.

Integrate with threat feeds

Arctic Hub can collect raw threat data from various threat feed providers. You can choose to activate both open source and commercial threat feeds.
