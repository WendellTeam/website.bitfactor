Arctic NODE

Arctic Node informs you what you need to fix

Watch video

Understand your cyber resilience

Arctic Node collects the cyber threat intelligence from Arctic Hub or other relevant sources and helps you understand the status of your cyber resilience.

Know what you need to fix

Whenever you get information on critical issues in your own network you know what you need to fix immediately. You can even automate some of the fixes by integrating Arctic Node to your existing security information and event management systems.

Avoid cyber threats

Arctic Node gives you information on cyber threats around you. You can use this information to make sure your network traffic, logs and security devices do not show indications of those threats. Integration to network security sensors [link to sensor feature text] helps you automate monitoring your network traffic.

Strengthen your defense cell

Users of Arctic Node can strengthen their defense cell [link to defense cell explanation on the front page] by validating the data shared by their Hub. For example, you can confirm a sighting of a malware URL or a command and control server.

Key Features

Integrate with threat feeds

Arctic Node can collect threat intelligence directly from a cyber security center or other central operator using Arctic Hub. In addition, you can choose to integrate different commercial or open source threat feeds in your Arctic Node. See the list of our threat feed partners. [link to About Us / Partners]

Understand your cyber resilience

Arctic Node dashboard shows you real-time information on the threats you have faced. This gives you a good overall picture on your cyber resilience and you can follow up on the trends how your cyber resilience has improved over time.

Integrate with SIEM

By integrating Arctic Node to your existing security information and event management systems you can improve the speed of fixing the critical issues in your network.
