We are looking for a DevOps oriented System Administrator and a Linux expert to join our team. As a member of our R&D team you will work between the product development and production, bringing our technology into use. As part of the Admin team, you will operate and maintain our Linux based infrastructure.

We use modern tools and we are continuously looking forward to improve and develop our methods, simultaneously maintaining good quality and stable operations. Due to the requirements that come from our customer base, you must pass security clearance in order to be employed.

What we are looking for:

* Profound interest and track record in secure system administration
* Strong Linux expertise
* DevOps orientation
* Experience in cloud infrastructure, preferably with AWS
* History with Docker and/or other containerization technologies
* Basic skills with regular scripting languages

What we offer:

* Grow with a team of experts in a disruptive cyber security company
* Relaxed but professional work environment
* Competitive salary
* Modern tools and systems

For more information, contact careers@arcticsecurity.com
